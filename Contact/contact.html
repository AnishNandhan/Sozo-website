FAQs

Explore our FAQs for quick answers to common queries about our platform.

When will SoZo launch?

SoZo is set to launch in Q3 of 2024.

Do I need a wallet to use SoZo?

No, you don’t need a separate wallet. SoZo’s login method includes a built-in wallet.

Do I need funds to pay for gas fees?

No, SoZo is completely gasless due to the reverse gas model of ICP.

Is SoZo a decentralized social media platform?

Yes, SoZo is a decentralized social media platform built entirely on the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP).

Do I need money to use SoZo?

No, while some features involve money, using SoZo is free of cost.

Do I need to pay to post on SoZo like on other decentralized social media platforms?

No, posting on SoZo is completely free.

Can I post NFTs bought from other marketplaces?

Yes, you can post NFTs bought from other marketplaces.

Can I post NFTs from other chains?

Not yet, but this feature is coming soon to SoZo.

Can I use internet identity to log in to SoZo?

No, we use NFID.
